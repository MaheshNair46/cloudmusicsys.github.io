# Privacy Policy for Nādevā

**Last Updated: May 2026**

Nādevā is an academic project developed for research purposes. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about any data processed.

### 1. Data We Collect
* **Account Information:** If you sign in via Google, we receive your name and email address to personalize your experience.
* **Usage Data:** We may track which songs are played to improve app performance.

### 2. How We Use Data
We use your data solely to provide music streaming services and for academic analysis within our project diary. We do not sell your data to third parties.

### 3. Storage
Your account metadata is stored securely in **Google Firebase**, and music files are hosted on **Oracle Cloud**.

### 4. Contact
For questions regarding this policy, please contact the development team via our college Oracle Academy portal.
